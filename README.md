# Description:

Mann & Kinn is a horror survival game centered around an ex-factory worker who wakes up trapped inside the abandoned factory where they once worked. The player’s objective is to survive the night and escape the maze-like building while being hunted by hostile mannequins. Throughout the game, players must search for useful items and tools to delay or avoid the monsters long enough to find the exit. Since the player has no weapons, the mannequins cannot be defeated, making stealth, strategy, and survival essential to gameplay. Failing to escape results in the player becoming a mannequin themselves.

For this project, we combined assets from the Unity Asset Store with custom-built models created by our team. To enhance the horror atmosphere and immersion, we implemented volumetric fog and environmental effects throughout the game. The mannequins use a “Weeping Angel”- style mechanic, in which they move and attack only when the player is not looking at them. When the player maintains eye contact, the mannequins remain frozen in place, creating suspenseful and intense gameplay moments.

# Future Improvements
For future improvements, we plan to expand the game’s environments with more detailed horror-themed decorations and additional maps to create a more immersive experience. We also aim to refine and complete existing scripts, implement a checkpoint respawn system that allows players to continue from their last location, and introduce more obstacles and gameplay complexity, including parkour elements and interactive challenges.

In addition, we plan to add a wider variety of tools and defensive items for players to use, along with different tiers of mannequins featuring unique behaviors and varying attack strengths to increase difficulty and replayability.
